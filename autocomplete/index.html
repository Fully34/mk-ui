Mk Autocomplete Examples

Checkout some simple setups and general funcioality. Be sure to turn on the console and watch the events fire!

For documentation check out our MkAutocomplete Documentation.

Single selection autocomplete.

Add a single symbol. Continuing to search results in the single selection being replaced by the new selection. We've removed the tags for this one as well.

Multi selection autocomplete.

Add up to 4 selections. Comma seperate entry to automatically add it to the selection list. Backspace to remove old entries.
